Our Story

A New Chapter in Medical Education

MNR Medical College was founded in 2026 at MNR Medicity Campus, Kolambe, Karnataka — a greenfield institution conceived from the ground up with a clear mission: equip future doctors with the knowledge, skills, and values to transform healthcare.

"We didn't inherit old infrastructure. We built everything new — so our students learn with the best, from day one."

Unlike institutions that grow incrementally, MNR launched its full academic portfolio simultaneously — MBBS, MD, MS, DM, MCh, and allied health programs — ensuring a rich, multi-level academic environment from the very first day.

With an attached teaching hospital right on campus, students experience real clinical rotations, live OT exposure, and patient care interactions that most colleges can only promise in later years.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Values

The principles that guide everything we do — from how we teach to how we care.

Vision

To be Karnataka's most respected medical institution — recognized for producing doctors who combine clinical excellence with deep compassion and ethical integrity.

Mission

To provide world-class medical education through modern infrastructure, research-driven teaching, and immersive clinical training — empowering students to lead healthcare transformation.

Values

Compassion in care. Integrity in practice. Excellence in learning. Innovation in research. These aren't slogans — they are the lived principles of every person at MNR.
