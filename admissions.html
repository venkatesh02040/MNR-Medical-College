Admission Process

How to Apply

A simple 4-step process to secure your seat at MNR Medical College.

01

Check Eligibility

Verify your NEET score, academic qualifications, and program-specific eligibility criteria before applying.

02

Submit Application

Fill the online application form with all required documents — academic certificates, NEET scorecard, ID proof.

03

Counselling & Verification

Attend the document verification and counselling session at the MNR Medicity Campus or online portal.

04

Confirm Your Seat

Pay the admission fee, complete enrollment formalities, and secure your seat in the inaugural 2026 batch.
